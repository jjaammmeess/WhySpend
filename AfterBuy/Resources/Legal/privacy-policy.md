## Who we are

**WhySpend** is provided by an developer.

- **We do not operate our own servers** to collect, store, or analyze your expense or mood entries  
- Data **stays on your iPhone by default**; only when you turn on **iCloud sync** does **Apple** sync it across devices signed into the **same Apple ID**  
- We **do not** sell your entries for advertising or share them with data brokers

## Storage, sync & retention

### On-device storage

By default, data is stored **locally on your iPhone** (SwiftData / local database).

### iCloud sync (optional)

If you enable sync under **Mine → Data Center → Cloud Sync**:

- Data syncs via **Apple iCloud / CloudKit** across devices signed into the **same Apple ID**  
- Sync uses **Apple’s** infrastructure; we **do not** route your entries through our own servers  
- With sync on, the system may use **silent remote notifications** to wake CloudKit background sync. These are delivered via Apple’s infrastructure and are **not** a developer-operated push or ad service  
- When sync is off, **new** data is not uploaded; existing cloud data follows Apple’s account policies  

### Retention

Data remains until you **delete entries**, **clear app data**, or **remove the App**. You may keep copies via in-app **JSON backup**.
