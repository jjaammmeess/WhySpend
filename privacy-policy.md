## Storage, sync & retention

### On-device storage

By default, data is stored **locally on your iPhone** (SwiftData / local database).

### iCloud sync (optional)

If you enable sync under **Mine → Data Center → Cloud Sync**:

- Data syncs via **Apple iCloud / CloudKit** across devices signed into the **same Apple ID**  
- Sync uses **Apple’s** infrastructure; we do not route your entries through our own servers  
- When sync is off, **new** data is not uploaded; existing cloud data follows Apple’s account policies  

### Retention

Data remains until you **delete entries**, **clear app data**, or **remove the App**. You may keep copies via in-app **JSON backup**.
